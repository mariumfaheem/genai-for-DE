**Start Date:** May 15, 2024

### 1. Position and Duties
1.1. The Employer agrees to employ the Employee in the position of Senior Software Engineer.
1.2. The Employee agrees to perform the duties and responsibilities associated with this position to the best of their abilities and to comply with all company policies and procedures.

### 2. Compensation
2.1. The Employee shall be paid an annual salary of $120,000, payable in accordance with the Employer's standard payroll practices.
2.2. The Employee shall be eligible for any standard benefits provided by the Employer, including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off, subject to the terms and conditions of the respective benefit plans.

### 3. Term of Employment
3.1. The Employee's employment with the Employer shall commence on August 15, 2024.
3.2. This Agreement shall continue until terminated by either party in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

### 4. Non-Compete Clause
4.1. During the term of this Agreement and for a period of one (1) year following the termination of the Employee's employment, the Employee agrees not to engage in any business or employment, directly or indirectly, that competes with the Employer's business.
4.2. The Employee acknowledges that this non-compete clause is reasonable in scope and necessary to protect the Employer's legitimate business interests.

### 5. Confidentiality
5.1. The Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary and confidential information of the Employer and not to disclose such information to any third party without the Employer's prior written consent.
5.2. This obligation of confidentiality shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

### 6. Termination
6.1. This Agreement may be terminated by either party at any time, with or without cause, by providing written notice to the other party.
6.2. Upon termination, the Employee shall return all company property, including documents and electronic files, to the Employer.

### 7. Governing Law
7.1. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas.

### 8. Entire Agreement
8.1. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter of this Agreement.
