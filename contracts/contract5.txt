**1. Employment and Duties**

Employer agrees to employ Samantha Green ("Employee") as a Software Development Manager. The Employee accepts employment with the Employer on the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement and agrees to devote her full time, attention, and efforts to the business and affairs of the Employer.

**2. Compensation**

(a) **Base Salary:** The Employer shall pay the Employee an annual salary of $120,000, payable in accordance with the Employer's standard payroll practices and subject to all appropriate withholdings and deductions.

(b) **Bonus:** The Employee shall be eligible for an annual performance bonus based on criteria established by the Employer. The terms of any such bonus will be determined at the discretion of the Employer.

**3. Benefits**

The Employee will be entitled to participate in any and all benefit programs that the Employer has established and maintains for its employees, subject to the terms and conditions of the applicable benefit plans.

**4. Term and Termination**

(a) **Term:** The term of this Agreement shall commence on August 15, 2024, and shall continue until terminated by either party in accordance with this Agreement.

(b) **Termination by Employer:** The Employer may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon providing the Employee with thirty (30) days written notice.

(c) **Termination by Employee:** The Employee may terminate this Agreement at any time upon providing the Employer with thirty (30) days written notice.

**6. Confidentiality**

The Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information and trade secrets of the Employer both during and after the term of employment.

**7. Miscellaneous**

(a) **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter of this Agreement.

(b) **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

(c) **Amendments:** This Agreement may be amended only by a written agreement signed by both parties.
